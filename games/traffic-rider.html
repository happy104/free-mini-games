About Traffic Rider

Traffic Rider is a first-person endless racing game that puts you on a motorbike for an exhilarating ride. With a full career mode, lifelike bike sounds, and detailed graphics, it redefines arcade racing. Speed through highways, overtake traffic, and upgrade your rides to conquer challenging missions. Embrace the thrill and master the art of smooth racing.

Features

  • First person camera view
  • 34 motorbikes to choose from
  • Real motor sounds recorded from real bikes
  • Detailed environments with day and night variations
  • Career mode with 90+ missions
  • Online leaderboards and 30+ achievements
  • Support for 19 languages

Controls

  • ↑ Throttle
  • ↓ Brake
  • ← Move left
  • → Move right
  • H Horn
  • Y Wheelie
  • T Look left
  • U Look right
  • R Gear up
  • F Gear down
  • P or F1 Pause

You can always change controls in the settings menu.
