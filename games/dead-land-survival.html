Dead Land: Survival is a survival horror game set in a zombie-infested apocalyptic world where you fight for survival with powerful skills you accumulate throughout gameplay. Fortify your shelter against relentless undead attacks and scavenge through desolate territories in search of valuable resources. Craft weapons, armor, and essential gear to give yourself an edge in these hostile lands. With danger around every corner, your aim is to keep calm and survive the end of the zombie apocalypse. How to Play Dead Land: Survival Set out on a treacherous quest through the desolate lands of Dead Land: Survival, where the world has fallen to ruin, and the infected ones lurk around every corner. Once-beautiful landscapes are now wastelands filled with relentless threats, stretching from the eerie remnants of your hometown, Nightstone, to the harsh quarantine zones. Each step into these dangerous territories demands courage as you fight tooth and nail to survive in an unforgiving, apocalyptic world. To stay alive, you must harvest resources from whatever nature has left behind. Gather essential materials from trees and other natural sources to upgrade your hero, repair broken vehicles, and restore key structures like your shelter and workshop. Loot is scattered throughout zombie-infested areas, and every scrap could be crucial to your survival. From metal to wood, each resource strengthens your chance of fighting back against the apocalypse. Combat is fierce as you face off against infected enemies with a range of weapons at your disposal. Craft melee weapons and guns, each designed for the brutal, turn-based struggles you’ll encounter. But survival takes more than brute force; you’ll need health packs and food to sustain your strength in prolonged battles. Weapons wear down over time, so be vigilant and repair them as needed, ensuring you're always ready for the next deadly encounter. Nightstone, your starting refuge in this bleak world, is home to fellow survivors who offer a glimmer of hope. Here, you’ll meet resilient characters who trade with you and assign quests that test your survival skills. You'll earn rewards as you complete these missions and unlock the chance to journey to other survivor outposts. Each quest brings you closer to understanding this dark world and forming alliances in a fight for humanity’s future. Engage in turn-based battles with zombies, craft powerful weapons and equipment, earn ratings to compete with clans, and complete a variety of quests in Dead Land: Survival.
