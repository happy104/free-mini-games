How to Play Battle Arena In a world overrun by demons, you must choose a region at a time to combat your opponent. In Battle Arena, your objective is pretty much to build and upgrade your heroes and compete in various challenges, particularly in the arena. The key to success in this game is to strategize by selecting the right battle heroes to maximize team effectiveness.
