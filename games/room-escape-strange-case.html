About Room Escape: Strange Case

Room Escape: Strange Case is a mystery puzzle game where you, the true detective, delve into the enigma of the elusive "Alchemist." Unravel the chilling tale of defiled graves, navigating through a unique graphic style and atmospheric puzzles. Encounter strange characters and escape clever traps in your quest to unveil the Alchemist's identity. Are you up for the challenge of this original and mysterious adventure? You can also watch the walkthrough for chapter 1, chapter 2, and chapter 3.
