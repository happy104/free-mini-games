About Mr. Racer

Mr Racer—Car Racing is a thrilling racing game in our driving category in which you zoom through busy streets in various locations. Drive at high speeds in a variety of stunning supercars, dodging traffic. Tackle a range of driving challenges and become the ultimate MR RACER. How to Play Mr Racer Start your racing journey with speed into the action! In Mr. Racer, there are various game modes that offer an exciting driving experience in a range of flashy automobiles. Speed your way through 5 unique locations, progressing your career as a racing driver and earning EXP to level up. Challenge. Complete various challenges to progress through the game. In challenge mode, there are 100 different challenges to complete, keeping the game fresh and offering tricky new tasks. Most of the challenges are time-based. You earn back some time by fast overtaking other vehicles. Race. In race mode, you’ll compete against another racer to place first. There are no bonuses for fast overtakes, so focus on beating your opponent to successfully complete the mission. Chase. When you unlock chase mode, you’ll enjoy an unlimited number of chases. Chase the opposition down and show them who the master racer is! Buy and upgrade new vehicles As you progress through the game and explore the various game modes on offer, you’ll earn money to buy new vehicles. There are various cars, some of which are incredibly fast. These newer vehicles will help you beat your opponents in races more easily. You can also upgrade your vehicles with new paint jobs, wheels, lights, and wheel stances. More Games Like This Are you looking for more supercar action after completing Mr. Racer? Check out more of our driving games or car games.
