Stickman Destruction 3 Heroes is a ragdoll physics action game. Crash the stickman into obstacles, send him tumbling down staircases and through walls into oblivious bystanders. Cause as much destruction as possible to earn the most gold! Cause Carnage Stickman Destruction 3 Heroes is all about inflicting high impact to your stickman and anything in his way. The results will vary depending on how much force you apply, combined with a spot of random luck. You can set your character in various starting positions to find out what causes the most chaos. Extra-high impacts happen in slow-motion for your amusement. Buy Upgrades The points you acquire at the end of the level accumulate. You can use your gold points to buy new vehicles that drastically increase your potential to do damage. You can even eject from the vehicle and go catapulting through walls and into other props. As well as buying vehicles, you can use your hard-earned gold to purchase levels, characters, and props to set up the perfect stickman apocalypse scenario for your entertainment.
